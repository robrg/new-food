Homemade Burgers with chips and broccoli

Macro's

  • Kcal: 302
  • Protein: 40g
  • Fat: 21g
  • Saturated Fats: 4g
  • Carbs: 53g
  • Sugar: 18g
  • Fibre: 6g
  • Salt 2.2g

Cooking Info:

  • Preperation Time: 20 Minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 Minutes
  • Serves: 1
  • Very Easy to Cook

Ingredients

  • 200/400g mince meat
  • 100g of chunky chips
  • 100/150g of broccoli
  • tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Half a red onion
  • burger sauce
  • cheese slices
  • Burger buns

This is another one of my favourite quick meals to make, if you're not worried about calories add bacon and an egg for that extra taste, and use broiche buns instead.

Step 1:

Grab all your mince and chuck it into a big bowl, then add a touch of olive oil, salt and pepper and mix in well. Then make the mince into small patties, you could also stuff cheese in the middle of the mince for that extra touch.

Step 2:

Next grab your desired amounts of chips and let them cook whilst cooking the broccoli for roughly 10 minutes.

Step 3:

Heat the pan on full and make sure its really hot before you start to cook, then grab your burgers and add another touch of olive oil and salt and pepper. Whilst they are cooking toast your buns and add the salad tomatoes, when the burgers are nearly done i add the cheese to them whilst still in the pan so they melt nicely around the meat. Once the burgers are done thats it, serve with your chips and broccoli.
