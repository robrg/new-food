Chicken Fajitas with Egg Fried Rice and Tortillas

Macro's

  • Kcal: 507
  • Protein: 40g
  • Fat: 21g
  • Saturated Fats: 4g
  • Carbs: 53g
  • Sugar: 18g
  • Fibre: 6g
  • Salt 2.2g

Cooking Info:

  • Preperation Time: 20 Minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 Hour
  • Serves: 2
  • Very Easy to Cook

Ingredients

  • 400g's of Chicken Breast
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 Tablespoon of Smoked Paprika
  • 100g's of Basmati Rice
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • Olive Oil
  • 20g's of Sugar
  • Red, Green and Yellow Peppers/li>
  • Red Onion
  • Srirachi Sauce
  • 2 Red Chillies Chopped
  • Ground Corriander
  • A dash of Sriracha
  • Salsa/Sour Cream

This is a classic Mexican Recipe i would be suprised if someone said they havent tried it before. It's really easy to make, its a great idea for a midweek meal.

Step 1:

If you have time let the Chicken Marinate with all the spices the night before or for a couple hours before cooking for the best results. If you forget then grab the Chicken Breasts cut them into even sized chunks and add them to a mixing bowl. Add some Olive Oil, chopped Garlic, Salt, Pepper, Chillies, and a sprinkle of Lime juice if you like it. Then finally add the ground Coriander, Smoked Paprika and Sriracha to the bowl and give it a good mix.

Step 2:

Next Chop all your Peppers and Onion and Fry that off, i like to add Sugar to caramalise the Veggies and some more Paprika. Whilst they are cooking you can start on the Rice, boil the rice until cooked and then add 2 eggs to the wok, season with Salt and Pepper and cook them until they are nearly cooked, then add the cooked rice and some soy sauce to the Wok, once the eggs are fully cooked you are done with the rice.

Step 3:

Make sure all the Chicken is thoroughly cooked all the way through and then serve with Tortillas, with Salsa and Sour Cream.
