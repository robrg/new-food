Fillet Steak with Potato's and Broccoli

Macro's

  • Kcal: 302
  • Protein: 40g
  • Fat: 21g
  • Saturated Fats: 4g
  • Carbs: 53g
  • Sugar: 18g
  • Fibre: 6g
  • Salt 2.2g

Cooking Info:

  • Preperation Time: 20 Minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 Minutes
  • Serves: 5 People
  • Very Easy to Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 Fillet Steak
  • 150/200g New potatoes
  • 100/150g of broccoli
  • Mustard
  • 2 Tablespoons of Mayonnaise
  • Half a red onion

This is probably my favourite meal, steak is a great source of protein and its so quick and easy to make, serve with potatoe salad or chips if you're feeling naughty.

Step 1:

Boil the new potatoes for 15-20 minutes and let them cool once they are cooked. Then cut the potatoes into small cubes and place them into a bowl. next chop the red onions and mix in the mayonnaise and mustard to the bowl, season with salt and pepper.

Step 2:

Then grab the steak out of the fridge and season with olive oil, salt and pepper on both sides. Leave the steak out of the fridge for about 20 minutes at room temperature as this will help with cooking. Then give the broccoli a quick wash and let it boil for roughly 10 minutes.

Step 3:

Heat the pan on full make sure it is really hot before you start to cook, then grab your steak and throw it in the pan, depending how you like your steak done it should only take 6-8 mins to cook, let the steak rest for a few minutes once its cooked.
