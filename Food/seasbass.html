Sea Bass Fillets with Chips and Broccoli

Macro's

  • Kcal: 302
  • Protein: 40g
  • Fat: 21g
  • Saturated Fats: 4g
  • Carbs: 53g
  • Sugar: 18g
  • Fibre: 6g
  • Salt 2.2g

Cooking Info:

  • Preperation Time: 20 Minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 Minutes
  • Serves: 1
  • Very Easy to Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 Fillets of Sea bass
  • 100g of chunky chips
  • 100/150g of broccoli
  • Lemon
  • Butter
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour

This is another easy dish to make that so Healthy for you, Fish is one of the best sources of protein and it will help you keep a nice lean figure without sacrificing taste!

Step 1:

First Start off by Marinating your Sea Bass, Place it in a bowl with some Olive Oil, Salt and Pepper and a squeeze of Lemon, make sure both sides are coated with seasoning. Then Place flour in one bowl and Buttermilk in the other. Dip a Fillet in the Buttermilk then into the flour, do the same for the other side and once you have done by of them leave them to one side.

Step 2:

Next grab your desired amounts of chips and let them cook whilst cooking the broccoli for roughly 10 minutes.

Step 3:

Heat the pan and grab your Sea bass, add a little Olive Oil to the pan and gently place them in the pan facing away from you and then season them again with Salt, Pepper, and Lemon. Cook both sides for around 4-5 minutes or until the skin on the bottom is nice and crispy. Once they are done thats it, serve with your chips and broccoli. You could also serve this with a side Salad.
